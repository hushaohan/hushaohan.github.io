Shaohan Hu

I am currently a PhD candidate, working in the Cyber Physical Computing Group @ the Department of Computer Science @ the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My advisor is Prof. Tarek F. Abdelzaher. My main research interest lies in designing and building mobile sensing / computing systems.
