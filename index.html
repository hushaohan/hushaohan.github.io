About me

I work at the Future Lab for Applied Research and Engineering (FLARE) at JPMorgan Chase & Co. as a research director. Our current projects span the area of quantum computing / communication, IoT, AR/VR, mobile and cloud computing. Prior to that, I spent a few years at IBM Research after getting my PhD in Computer Science from UIUC.
