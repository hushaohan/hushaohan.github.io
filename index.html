Shaohan Hu

I have joined IBM Research, where I am a member of the Mobile Enterprise Software group. I received my Ph.D. from UIUC CS, where I worked in the Cyber Physical Computing Group with Prof. Tarek Abdelzaher. My research interests lie in the general areas of cyber-physical systems, mobile ubiquitous computing, crowd and social sensing systems, big data analysis and cloud computing systems.
